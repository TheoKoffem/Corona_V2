General information about the novel corona-virus.

The novel corona-virus (COVID-19), is a fast spreading virus with mild symptoms for most people. It has an incubation period of 5.1 days and after 11.5 days symptoms will start to appear. During the incubation period it is possible (but uncommon) to infect other people, but it is during the period when a person is showing symptoms that they will spread the disease the most. The mortality-rate is 3.4%

The symptoms are as following:
  • Fever
  • Fatigue
  • Dry cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Sore throat*
  • Runny nose*
  • Sneezing*
  • Diarrhea*
  • Decreased kidneys function*
  • Decreased white blood cells*
*Uncommon
